# 🏆 Rank Builder AI

> AI-powered question paper generator for Indian competitive exam aspirants

🌐 **Live at:** [rankbuilderai.shop]

---

## 📌 What is Rank Builder AI?

Rank Builder AI is a web platform designed to help students preparing for 
competitive exams like **JEE, NEET, and CET** build conceptual understanding 
through personalised practice.

The core idea is simple: the more problems a student solves on a specific 
topic, the stronger their conceptual foundation becomes. Rank Builder AI 
makes that process easy, fast, and structured.

---

## ✨ Features

- 📝 **Custom Question Paper Generator** — Select subject and topic, get 
  a tailored question paper instantly
- 🎯 **Difficulty Selection** — Choose between JEE, NEET, CET, and more
- 📄 **PDF Download** — Download question papers with detailed solutions 
  directly to your device
- ⚡ **Instant Mock Test** — Take a live mock test directly on the platform
- 👤 **Student Profiles** — Create and manage your own study profile
- 🧠 **Concept-Based Learning** — Built around strengthening conceptual 
  understanding, not just rote practice

## 🚀 How to Use

1. Visit the website
2. Create your student profile
3. Select your subject and topic
4. Choose your exam level (JEE / NEET / CET)
5. Generate your question paper or start an instant mock test
6. Download PDF with solutions or complete the test online
