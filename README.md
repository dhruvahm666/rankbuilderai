## 📌 What is Rank Builder AI?

Rank Builder AI is a web platform designed to help students preparing for competitive exams like **JEE, NEET, and CET** build conceptual understanding through personalised practice.

The core idea is simple: the more problems a student solves on a specific topic, the stronger their conceptual foundation becomes. Rank Builder AI makes that process easy, fast, and structured.

---

## ✨ Features

| Feature | Description |
|---|---|
| 📝 Custom Question Paper Generator | Select subject and topic, get a tailored question paper instantly |
| 🎯 Difficulty Selection | Choose between JEE, NEET, CET, and more |
| 📄 PDF Download | Download question papers with detailed solutions directly to your device |
| ⚡ Instant Mock Test | Take a live mock test directly on the platform |
| 👤 Student Profiles | Create and manage your own study profile |
| 🧠 Concept-Based Learning | Built around strengthening conceptual understanding, not just rote practice |

## 🚀 How to Use

> **Step 1** — Visit [rankbuilderai.shop](https://rankbuilderai.shop)

> **Step 2** — Create your student profile to get started

> **Step 3** — Select your subject and the specific topic you want to practice

> **Step 4** — Choose your exam level — JEE, NEET, or CET

> **Step 5** — Hit generate to instantly get a tailored question paper, or jump into a live mock test

> **Step 6** — Download the PDF with detailed solutions, or submit your mock test and review your score
